Breaking Silence (2013)(Install)(Sample)

Following on from Broken Silence (2012), Breaking Silence was an audio investigation Aleksandrow undertook during his Masters of Applied Design & Art at Curtin University. It is comprised of 90 separate breakages, each on their own track, separated into 5 playlists. The resulting installation allows for a unique sequence of breaks with each experience of the work.
